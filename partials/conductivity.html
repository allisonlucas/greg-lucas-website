WACCM-GEC uses the methodology outlined in Baumgaertner's 2013 paper to generate the production and loss of ions, which includes radon, galactic cosmic rays, and solar proton events, to determine atmospheric conductivity. Baumgaertner's 2014 paper is used to more accurately model the loss of ions due to their attachment to water droplets within clouds.
