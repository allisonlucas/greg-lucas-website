The Global Electric Circuit is a research field that investigates the electrical connections within Earth's atmosphere. Greg has developed a GEC model that is incorporated into a community climate model which provides the physical representation of the atmosphere. A full description of this new model named WACCM-GEC is published in JGR-Atmospheres. This work was selected for a highlight in EOS.

Utilizing a climate model to produce a physical atmosphere enables a realistic representation of the electric currents within the atmosphere to be produced. To provide a comprehensive GEC model, a global distribution of conductivity and source strengths must be calculated.
